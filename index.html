What is Elder Scrolls Online?

MMORPG

ESO is a massive multiplayer online roleplaying game. This means you can see other players in real time! You can ignore them and go about your game, or group up, help them defeat monsters, talk, travel together and more!

Series

Released in 2014, ESO is the most recent PC/Console installment in the Elder Scrolls series, following Skyrim in 2011.

Huge Player Base

In 2017, ESO had over 2.5 million active players!

Choose How to Play

With the release of Tamriel Unlimited, gameplay in ESO is mostly non-linear. If you have access to the relevant DLC content, all areas are available to you immediately. There's currently eleven area expansions and even more dungeons. That's a lot of places to explore!

Races

You can choose from 9 playable races (or 10 if you buy the Imperial upgrade). Will you utilise the Khajiit's natural talent for sneaking to become a notorious thief? The High Elf's aptitude for magic to be a powerful sorcerer? Or maybe become a Nord, hardened from a life in Tamriel's toughest climates and able to survive any challenge? Don't worry, you can create 9 characters, so you don't even have to choose!

Classes

There are 6 classes available, ranging from healers to damage dealers to tanks. Will you be a Nightblade, masters of shadows and subterfuge? Or a Templar, with powerful healing spells ready to lend a hand to those in need? Or perhaps a Necromancer, feared and shunned with an army of the Undead?

Tamriel

ESO is set in the fictional land of Tamriel. There are two main crises at hand: the battle for the Ruby Throne - to control the land; and the battle to save Tamriel from Molag Bal's invasion, a Daedric Lord who has placed the fate of the world in the balance. However, each land is suffering its own conflicts, and it's down to you, the player, to decide who wins and who loses.

What is Elder Scrolls Online?

the elder scrolls online is a mmorpg (massive multiplayer online role-playing game). released in 2014 by the folks over at Bethesda, it is the most recent major platform release in the Elder Scrolls universe it is continually added to year on year, currently boasting eleven area expansions and several harder-than-base-game group dungeons which coincide with the year's release

Who We Are

a group of predominantly elder scrolls online players who have met through in-game means over the years and have been gaming together ever since. a mix of streamers and casual players based out of the Gay Street Gamers building, our events are a mix of on-location gamers and PlayStation players from all around the EU. you can come see us in action, or watch one of our squad streams on Twitch.

What We're Doing

getting into an mmorpg can be a daunting experience, so we hope to educate newer players through our "it's okay to be a noob" sessions ESO is a massive game, even seasoned players may not always know what to do/how to do it. so, we'll be hosting set showcases and reviews, dungeon and trial runs and more but, ultimately, we're here to have fun - join us as we role-play as heroes and heroines, murderers and assassins, famers and housing-enthusiasts and much more
