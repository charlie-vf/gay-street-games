What is Elder Scrolls Online?

the elder scrolls online is a mmorpg (massive multiplayer online role-playing game). released in 2014 by the folks over at Bethesda, it is the most recent major platform release in the Elder Scrolls universe it is continually added to year on year, currently boasting eleven area expansions and several harder-than-base-game group dungeons which coincide with the year's release

Who We Are

a group of predominantly elder scrolls online players who have met through in-game means over the years and have been gaming together ever since. a mix of streamers and casual players based out of the Gay Street Gamers building, our events are a mix of on-location gamers and PlayStation players from all around the EU. you can come see us in action, or watch one of our squad streams on Twitch.

What We're Doing

getting into an mmorpg can be a daunting experience, so we hope to educate newer players through our "it's okay to be a noob" sessions ESO is a massive game, even seasoned players may not always know what to do/how to do it. so, we'll be hosting set showcases and reviews, dungeon and trial runs and more but, ultimately, we're here to have fun - join us as we role-play as heroes and heroines, murderers and assassins, famers and housing-enthusiasts and much more
