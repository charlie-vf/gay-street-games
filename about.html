What We're Doing

Teaching

We understand that open world games can be quite daunting, especially when there's other players everywhere. We want people to be able to enjoy ESO as much as we do, so our events are detailed and informative so you, too, can explore Tamriel and conquer the hardest enemies.

Creating

There's so much to do in ESO, not just fighting monsters. There's hundreds of outfit styles and colors to find and earn; thousands of furniture items to make your home your own; fun titles to show others what you can do; and so much more. We've all created a personal experience in our own play throughs of the game, and we want you to be able to do the same!

Having Fun

Ultimately, we're just a group of friends having a good time. We stream, write articles and host events, but we're far from professionals. Sit back, relax, and watch us wreak havoc across the continent of Tamriel.
